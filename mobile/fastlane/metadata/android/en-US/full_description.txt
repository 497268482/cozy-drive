Cozy Cloud makes the personal cloud more accessible. Files and Photos you keep in your Cozy, your personnal cloud are accessible from everywhere.

Cozy Files allow you to:
    - Access the files you have in your personal cloud
    - Automatically synchronize your phone or tablet's photos with you Cozy

Respectful of the user's privacy, Cozy Cloud simplifies daily tasks: synchronize and backup photos, automate bills downloading, manage expenses, etc. Flexible and easy to use, our Open Source platform allow users to create and develop their own applications.

We'd love to hear from you! Please use Github issues to report bugs and vote for next features : https://github.com/cozy/cozy-files-v3/issues
